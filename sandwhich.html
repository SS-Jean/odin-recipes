Ingredients

  • 1 round bread loaf
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons yellow mustard
  • 2 cups shredded iceberg lettuce
  • 1/2 pound sliced deli ham
  • 1/2 pound sliced deli turkey
  • 6 slices Swiss cheese
  • 6 slices Cheddar or Colby Jack cheese

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2

Cut a wide circle out of the bread loaf top like a soup bowl.

Step 3

Hollow out the bread without breaking through the sides.

Step 4

Melt butter with mustard for 15 seconds in a bowl.

Step 5

Brush the butter mixture on the loaf and top; place on baking sheet.

Step 6

Bake for 10 minutes. Let it cool.

Step 7

Layer lettuce, cheese, ham, and turkey inside the loaf.

Step 8

Wrap sandwich and weigh it down with another baking sheet.

Step 9

Refrigerate for 1 hour or overnight.

Step 10

Serve whole or slice into triangle pieces.
